Privacy Policy for Focus Grade Simulator
=========================================
Last updated: May 7th, 2025

Information Collection
---------------------
The Focus Grade Simulator extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations and hypothetical grade simulations are performed locally within your browser and are not saved or shared with any third parties.

Website Access
-------------
This extension only runs on Focus School Software gradebook pages as specified in the permissions. It does not access any other websites or your browsing history.

Data Storage
-----------
The extension uses Chrome's storage API only to temporarily store:
- Your hypothetical assignments for the current session
- Assignment categories from your gradebook

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Updates
-------
This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
