# Focus Grade Calculator

A Chrome extension that lets students simulate hypothetical assignments and instantly see how they impact their grade in the Focus Gradebook system.

## Features

- **Add hypothetical assignments** with earned and total points
- **Weighted and unweighted** grading modes with auto-detection
- **Instant grade updates** based on what-if scenarios
- **GPA Calculator** for projected GPAs based on current grades
- **Multiple themes** to customize the popup appearance
- **Undo/Redo** for easy management of hypothetical assignments
- **100% local** — no data ever leaves your browser

## Install

Available on the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/focus-grade-calculator/kkgjbhcmfnlondneafpkagemnjpiomka).

## How to Use

### Grade Calculator
1. Open your Focus gradebook in any class
2. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
3. Enter assignment details (points earned, points possible, category)
4. Click "Add Assignment" to see your recalculated grade

### GPA Calculator
1. Navigate to your main grades page in Focus
2. Click the extension icon and select "GPA Calculator"
3. Review auto-selected classes and click "Calculate GPA"

## Privacy

- All calculations happen locally in your browser
- No data is collected, stored, or transmitted
- No personal information is accessed
- Only activates on Focus gradebook pages
